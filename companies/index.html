How to Use This Directory: Use this list as a starting point for your insurance search. Contact each carrier through a licensed independent agent — independent agents can compare multiple carriers simultaneously and often have access to programs not available directly. For hard-to-place risks (wildfire zones, large commercial operations, unique facilities), ask your agent about surplus lines markets.

How to Choose a Horse Property Insurer

  • Financial strength — verify AM Best ratings of A- or better for any carrier you consider
  • State availability — not all carriers write in all states; confirm availability in your state
  • Farm policy experience — carriers with dedicated agricultural divisions understand horse property risks better than general homeowners carriers
  • Claims reputation — research claims handling reputation through your agent and online reviews
  • Coverage breadth — compare what is included vs. what requires endorsements across carriers
  • Premium competitiveness — obtain at least 3 quotes; farm insurance pricing varies significantly between carriers for identical risks
