Ways to Lower Your Premium

🔥
Install Fire Detection
Monitored smoke and heat detection in barns can reduce premiums 5–15% with most carriers.
🏗️
Upgrade to Metal Roofing
Class 4 impact-resistant metal roofing qualifies for hail discounts in tornado-prone states.
🌿
Maintain Defensible Space
Documented fire mitigation supports coverage in wildfire zones and may reduce premiums.
📋
Raise Your Deductible
Increasing from $1,000 to $5,000 can reduce premiums 10–20%. Self-insure small losses.
🤝
Bundle Policies
Placing farm and auto with the same carrier typically produces 5–15% multi-policy discounts.
🏆
Maintain Clean Claims History
Avoid filing small claims. A clean 5-year history is one of the most valuable premium reducers.
